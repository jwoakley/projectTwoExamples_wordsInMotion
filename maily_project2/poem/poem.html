Finding My Community

by Mai Ly Torres Baker

A found sense of community
When we gather together sharing laughter, food, and stories.
We build feelings of belonging and unity as we
Listen to, care for, and create with each other.

When we gather together sharing laughter, food, and stories
We find uplifting support within one another.
Listening, caring, and creating
A space to establish powerful rapport.

Finding uplifting support within one another,
We begin to connect across generations and cultures.
A space to establish powerful rapport and
Create new memories that will cultivate feelings of nostalgia.

We begin to connect across generations and cultures,
Taking the opportunity to find purpose and
Create new memories that will cultivate feelings of nostalgia.
I have found community.
